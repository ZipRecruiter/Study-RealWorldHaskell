The Bunch of Carrots Public House

Nestled in the beautiful Hereford countryside in the UK is the only pub in the world with the word carrots in the title. It is named after a rock formation close by which, in its day resembled a bunch of carrots. 

A large friendly pub with a seven day carvery - offering a great selection of roast meats all sourced from high quality local Herefordshire suppliers. With a large range of other dishes, every taste is catered for.  And of course carrots are always on the menu!!

The City of Hereford is only three miles down the road, with its interesting shops and ancient streets.

Highlight of the Carrot Museum's visit was to sample the Bunch of Carrots Ale, brewed specially by the brewery solely for this establishment. A great pint!

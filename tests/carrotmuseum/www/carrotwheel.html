The Giant Carrot Circle

Memorial Art Gallery Rochester NY in 1993

This Carrot Wheel was a giant circle of carrots displayed on the wall.

There was nothing too visually stunning about this work, but the concept behind it is fascinating - "Carrot Wheel" was composed by the Art Guys as a biodegradable work of art. In order for the composition to remain intact the carrots need to be replaced regularly, resulting in a different concept of "worth."

For the "Carrot Wheel," time is not something that increases its value, but rather the concept in and of itself.

Roots in South Carolina

Jennifer Roberts - collector and another carrot tattoo! - is there no end to the carrot collection craze?

She actually started collecting by accident but really glad she did! She collected  'bunnies' and her best friend used to call her Jennie Bunnie, which she hated, so told her that she wanted to collect something more unusual because, well, she IS unusual, nay, unique and wanted that part of her personality to shine through.  Well, what do bunnies eat? Carrots of course!

Jennifer has a lot in her collection, and below is a sample put together specially for the Carrot Museum.   Favourite items include pottery pieces and folk art pieces.

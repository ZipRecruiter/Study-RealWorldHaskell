The Carrot Granny

Diana Kukk is a relatively new collector with 22 pieces in her collection. She started collecting in May 2003 and was inspired after visiting Romana's "Carrot Museum" collection in Rhode Island.  Click here to see that.

Diana operates a bed and breakfast establishment in the waterfront Town of Onancock located on the Eastern Shore of Virginia.

This busy carrot collector also works part-time as a court reporter. Her favourite pieces are the carrot salt and pepper shakers.

When not playing with her nine grandchildren who also love her carrot collection she also enjoy manages to fit in  kayaking in the Onancock Creek!

Another fine addition to the Carrot Collectors Club.

Here is a picture of her favourite carrot items, the set of salt and pepper shakers that are the oldest, perhaps 1950s vintage.

Diana also finds time to collect Early American antiques.

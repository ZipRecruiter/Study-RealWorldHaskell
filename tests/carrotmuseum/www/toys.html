KATIE CARROT by Hasbro Toys

Her  "Home"

Katie in the "flesh"

Born in 1952, Mr Potato Head was the first toy to be advertised on national TV.

Katie Carrot appeared as a stand alone version in late 1964, soon after Mr Potato Head had been developed for use with his own plastic body.  This was introduced a concerns grew about hygiene and the sharply pointed body part previously used to pierce a potato was deemed inappropriate. (Source - Play Museum, Rochester, NY)

 Winnie the Pooh: "Pooh Counting Carrots" - produced by Tiger Electronics

An Electronic Learning Toy which has 4 modes of play:

Find, Identify, What Comes Next and Counting. Press a number and the carrots will light up and Pooh says "Well hello there".

It occupies kids for hours and is a well respected and admired learning tool. The first thing kids turn to at any Museum show!

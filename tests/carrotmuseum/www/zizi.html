The Carrot Tattoo Lady - Zizi

Zizi Fouquet-Howell from California is a true carrot lady. Not only does she have a comprehensive collection of carrot memorabilia but is also festooned with carrot tattoos, 35 carrots in all!.

More evidence of real carrot madness.

Zizi isn't addicted to carrots, but she is rather obsessed with them. She has 35 carrots tattooed across her body, and more than 1,000 carrot-themed items in her home which include pillows, candles, soaps and a $4000 carrot dresser that holds her mother's ashes.

She even takes it a step further, taking her carrots with her to a punk rock concert by a performer named appropriately Carrot Top -- but not that one. She even uses carrots as hair rollers, all in her attempt to have more carrots than anyone else in the world.

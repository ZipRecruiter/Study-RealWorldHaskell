Springtime Live event in Yorkshire

April 2014 (and subsequently)

Welcome to Springtime Live, a brand new event which first took place in April 2014 - hosted by the organisers of the Great Yorkshire Show and held on the Great Yorkshire Showground, Harrogate.  Event website here.

Suitable for families with young children, there was a whole host of hands on activities and workshops based on a Spring, new life and rural theme. All activities at Springtime Live are FREE, you just need to buy your tickets to get in.

The Carrot Museum has a series of information displays about the history of carrots, their nutritional properties and the colours and shapes of carrots. Activities include making a carrot bag, planting carrot seed to take home; various puzzles, games and colouring in for carrot fans of all ages. Also plenty of souvenir giveaways like postcards and badges, and instructional leaflets/recipes.

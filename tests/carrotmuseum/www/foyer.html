Welcome world wide web traveller to the World Carrot Museum, dedicated to telling the fascinating story of the wonderful Carrot.

It is the first virtual museum in the world entirely devoted to the history, evolution, science, sociology and art of Carrots. The mission is to educate, inform and amuse visitors through the discovery, collection, preservation, interpretation and exhibition of objects relating to the Carrot. This site provides lots of interesting and useful information about the humble carrot. 

The Museum is divided into these main areas of interest:  History, Wild Carrot, the Carrot today, Nutrition, Cultivation, Recipes, Trivia,  Cooking advice and more fun stuff than you ever realised existed about the ubiquitous carrot.

You will also find some serious academic research and interpretation.
